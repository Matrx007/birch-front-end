BIRCH

- your life, but tight & organized

Our goal is to simplify our lives. By having everything in one place, you can find information easier, make connections and have them synchronize across all of your devices.

Birch combines what otherwise would be multiple different apps: notes, music player, cross-platform file syncing, document editor, graph creator and so on. Birch can also be extended with extensions written in JavaScript so the possibilities are really endless.
